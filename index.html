OpenEconomicData is an independent, open-source platform that archives, monitors, and triangulates official labor-market and economic statistics to surface anomalies, provide second opinions, and rebuild public trust.

More is coming soon.

About the project

Recent concerns about political pressure on official statistics have revealed the need for transparency, redundancy, and independent verification. This platform will:

  • Archive official releases and track revisions over time.
  • Flag unusual patterns or metadata shifts in context.
  • Triangulate official numbers with alternative data sources.
  • Offer delayed independent replication using public microdata.
  • Publish all code and provenance openly.
